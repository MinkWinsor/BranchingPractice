Steven's (plagiarized) Recipe for Oreo Cookies

Cookies:
1 cup granulated sugar
1 cup butter or margarine
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
3/4 cup cocoa powder

Filling:
1/2 cup butter
2 tbsp half and half
1 tsp vanilla
Pinch of salt
3-3 1/2 cups powdered sugar

Heat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.

Cream butter and sugar. Add egg and vanilla. Sift together flour, cocoa, baking powder, and salt.
Add dry mixture to butter mixture, and mix well.

Divide dough in half, wrap it, and refrigerate.
Once chilled, roll out dough and cut out with cookie cutter.
Bake 10-13 minutes.

Filling:
Combine all ingredients except sugar.

Add sugar slowly, mix well.

Use a piping bag to pipe onto cookies to make oreos.
